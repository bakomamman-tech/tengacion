01 / Investment thesis

The opportunity

A transaction-enabled community platform, not only a social feed.

Tengacion combines social networking, creator discovery, digital content monetization, marketplace transactions, and AI-assisted engagement in one Nigerian-built platform.

  • Foreign platforms prioritize global advertising more than local creator payments and direct fan monetization.
  • Social selling is fragmented across chats, informal DMs, multiple networks, and disconnected payment flows.
  • Emerging creators struggle to earn consistently because discovery, engagement, and payments live in separate products.
  • Few credible local platforms combine community, entertainment, creator monetization, and commerce.
Users create attention. Creators convert attention into paid work and fan support. Sellers turn trusted discovery into transactions. One connected economic loop

Creator-led growth

Public fan pages, releases, subscriptions, and shareable catalogs pull audiences into the network.

Multiple revenue paths

Creator commissions, marketplace fees, premium tools, promoted posts, sponsorships, and future advertising.

Africa-first execution

Local payment behavior, mobile performance, trust workflows, and culturally relevant discovery shape the product.

04 / Revenue model

Diversified by design

Near-term transaction revenue, with recurring and advertising upside.

The strongest early revenue comes from creator purchases, subscriptions, and marketplace transactions. Promotion and advertising scale after user activity grows.

Creator digital sales 40%

Platform commission on music, books, podcasts, and subscriptions. High-margin digital products with direct fan demand.

Marketplace fee ₦300

Per successful purchase. Simple social-commerce monetization as approved seller activity grows.

Promoted posts ₦2k–₦25k

Per campaign. Creators and SMEs pay for targeted visibility inside active communities.

Premium creator tools ₦2,500

Per active creator each month. Recurring SaaS-like revenue for serious creators.

Ads & sponsorships CPM / CPC

Traffic-scale upside that can be introduced without weakening the core creator-commerce model.

High-margin inventory

The marginal cost of selling an additional digital release is low relative to physical commerce.

Revenue follows activity

Creators, fans, buyers, and sellers create multiple transaction moments across one network.

Reduced ad dependence

Tengacion can build meaningful revenue before a large-scale advertising business is required.
